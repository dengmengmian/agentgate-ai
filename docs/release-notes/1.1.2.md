## AgentGate 1.1.2 — Auto-restart after updates / 更新后自动重启

### What's new
- **AgentGate restarts itself after an update installs.** No more manually quitting and reopening the app.

### 本次更新
- **更新安装完成后自动重启。** 不再需要手动 quit + 重开。

### Why it matters / 为什么重要
One less step between an update and getting back to work. 更新即用，少一步操作。
