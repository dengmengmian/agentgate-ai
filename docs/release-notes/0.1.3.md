## AgentGate 0.1.3 — Switch Codex safely, without losing your sessions / 安全切换 Codex，不丢会话

### What's new
- **One-click Codex switching.** "Switch to official / Switch to AgentGate" saves and restores both `config.toml` and `auth.json` as a set.
- **Your official sessions are preserved.** Applying AgentGate keeps your original OAuth tokens, so switching back to official doesn't lose your chat history.
- **Pollution warning.** If an old version overwrote your `OPENAI_API_KEY`, AgentGate shows a yellow warning.

### 本次更新
- **Codex 一键切换。** 「切换到官方 / 切换到 AgentGate」整体保存恢复 `config.toml` + `auth.json`。
- **保留官方会话。** 应用 AgentGate 时保留原始 OAuth tokens，切回官方时对话记录不丢。
- **污染检测警告。** 检测到 `OPENAI_API_KEY` 被旧版覆盖时弹黄色警告。

### Why it matters / 为什么重要
Makes switching Codex between official and AgentGate reversible and safe — no lost sessions, no surprise key overwrites. 让 Codex 在官方和 AgentGate 之间切换变得可逆、安全，不丢会话、不被意外覆盖密钥。
